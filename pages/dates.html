Obed-Edom is returning to live shows on June 26th, 2021 with a pagan-inspired Midsommar show at Leigh Acres, an outdoor farm venue located 15 minutes from downtown Huntsville. You can purchase tickets ahead of time for $7 here. Tickets are also available for $10 at the door.

This event will feature traditional midsummer-style festivities with fire, maypole dancing, food, vendors, and music. This event is BYOB and camping spots and other overnight accomodations are available by arrangement (directly with Leigh Acres).

Attendees are encouraged to wear white or floral clothing and bring an acoustic or percussive intrument to join in on group improvised folkmusic.

Doors open at 4. Amplified music will run until 10:30 pm.
